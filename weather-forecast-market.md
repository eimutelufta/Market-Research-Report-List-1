<p><ul><li>Short-range Forecasting</li><li>Medium-range Forecasting</li><li>Long-range Forecasting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weather forecasting markets can be categorized into three main types: short-range, medium-range, and long-range forecasting. Short-range forecasting provides accurate weather predictions typically within 1 to 3 days, focusing on immediate weather conditions. Medium-range forecasting spans 3 to 10 days, offering insights into upcoming weather patterns and trends. Long-range forecasting covers periods beyond 10 days, often extending to seasonal predictions, analyzing broader climatic factors to assess potential weather changes and impacts, though with less precision than shorter-range forecasts.</p></p>
