<p><p>The process pumps market consists of various types, including axial flow pumps, centrifugal pumps, multistage pumps, and others. Axial flow pumps move fluid parallel to the pump shaft, ideal for low-pressure applications. Centrifugal pumps are widely used for their efficiency in transferring fluids at high speeds. Multistage pumps increase pressure by using multiple impellers, suitable for high-pressure applications. Other pumps can include diaphragm, gear, or peristaltic types, catering to specific industrial needs and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
