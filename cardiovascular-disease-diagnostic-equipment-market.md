<p><strong>The Cardiovascular Disease Diagnostic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Vitro Diagnostics</li><li>In Vivo Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular disease diagnostic equipment market is divided into two main types: in vitro diagnostics and in vivo diagnostics. In vitro diagnostics involve analyzing blood, urine, or other samples outside the body to detect biomarkers, enabling early disease identification and monitoring. In vivo diagnostics, on the other hand, involve real-time imaging or monitoring techniques, such as echocardiograms or angiography, performed directly within the body. Both types play crucial roles in accurate diagnosis and effective management of cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
