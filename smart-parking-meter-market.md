<p><p>The Smart Parking Meter market comprises two main types: Single Space and Multi Space meters. Single Space meters are designed for individual parking spots, providing real-time payment and availability information for each space, enhancing user convenience. In contrast, Multi Space meters serve multiple parking spaces, allowing users to park in any designated area and pay at a centralized unit. This type often features advanced functionalities like credit card processing and mobile app integration, streamlining the parking experience for both users and operators.</p></p>
