<p><ul><li>Direct Spray Cooling</li><li>Immersion Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Contact Liquid Cooling market encompasses two main types: Direct Spray Cooling and Immersion Cooling. Direct Spray Cooling involves the application of liquid directly onto hot components, enhancing heat dissipation while minimizing airflow limitations. Immersion Cooling, on the other hand, submerges entire server components in thermally conductive liquids, facilitating efficient heat transfer and enabling higher cooling capacities. Both methods aim to improve energy efficiency and performance in data centers, addressing the growing demand for effective thermal management solutions.</p></p>
