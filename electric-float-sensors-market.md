<p><p>The electric float sensors market primarily features two types: top-mounted and side-mounted sensors. Top-mounted float sensors are installed at the tank's top and measure liquid levels through a floating mechanism that rises and falls with the liquid. They are ideal for larger tanks and applications with limited access. In contrast, side-mounted float sensors are mounted on the tank's side, offering a compact design suitable for smaller tanks. Both types are crucial for continuous level monitoring in diverse industries, including water treatment and manufacturing.</p></p>
