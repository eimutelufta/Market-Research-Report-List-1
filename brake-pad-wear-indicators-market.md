<p><p>Brake pad wear indicators are crucial components in vehicle safety, providing vital information about brake performance. The market comprises two main types: electrical indicators and audible indicators. Electrical indicators utilize sensors to detect wear levels and can illuminate dashboard warnings when pads are close to replacement, offering real-time feedback. Audible indicators, on the other hand, feature built-in wear sensors that create sound alerts when pads are worn down, alerting drivers with noticeable noises. Both enhance vehicle safety and maintenance awareness.</p></p>
