<p><strong>The Semiconductor Test Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe Card</li><li>Load Board</li><li>Burn-inBoard(BIB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor test board market comprises various types, including probe cards, load boards, and burn-in boards (BIB). Probe cards facilitate efficient testing of integrated circuits by connecting test equipment to semiconductor wafers. Load boards support functional testing by providing the necessary connections for device power and signals during validation. Burn-in boards (BIB) are used for reliability testing, subjecting chips to elevated temperatures and voltages to detect early failures, ensuring product quality before market release. Each type plays a crucial role in semiconductor testing.</p></p>
