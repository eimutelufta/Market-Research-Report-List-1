<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The block paving/paver sealer market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based sealers. Solvent-based sealers penetrate deeply into the pavers, providing strong protection against stains and enhancing color, but they can emit strong odors and require careful application. Water-based sealers, on the other hand, are environmentally friendly, low in VOCs, and easier to apply, making them popular among DIYers. Both types serve to protect and maintain the appearance of paved surfaces, catering to different user preferences and application needs.</p></p>
