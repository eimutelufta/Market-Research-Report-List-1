<p><ul><li>Dispersing agent</li><li>Anti-foam agent</li><li>Rheology control agent</li><li>Leveling agent</li><li>Adhesion promoter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coating additives enhance paint performance and application properties. Dispersing agents improve pigment distribution and stability. Anti-foam agents prevent bubble formation during application and drying. Rheology control agents modify viscosity for better flow and leveling. Leveling agents ensure smooth finishes by minimizing imperfections. Adhesion promoters enhance the bond between the coating and substrate, preventing peeling or flaking. Other additives may include preservatives, drying agents, and UV stabilizers, each tailored to improve specific attributes of coatings.</p></p>
