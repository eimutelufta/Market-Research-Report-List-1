<p><p>Free-space optical communications technology encompasses several key market components. Transmitters convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission through the air. Receivers capture these optical signals and convert them back into electrical form. Modulators adjust the properties of the optical signals to encode information, while demodulators decode the received signals for accurate data interpretation. Additionally, the market includes various other components such as optical lenses and amplifiers that enhance signal quality and transmission distance, supporting diverse applications.</p></p>
