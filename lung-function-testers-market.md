<p><p>The lung function testers market includes various devices used to assess respiratory health. **Spirometers** measure airflow and lung capacity. **Breath CO monitors** detect carbon monoxide levels in breath, indicating exposure to smoke or air pollutants. **Oscillometers** assess airway resistance through forced oscillation technique. **Ergospirometers** combine exercise testing with lung function assessment, evaluating oxygen consumption during physical activity. **Dose-controlled drug nebulizers** deliver specific medication doses via inhalation, while **Others** encompass additional devices used for pulmonary function testing and monitoring.</p></p>
