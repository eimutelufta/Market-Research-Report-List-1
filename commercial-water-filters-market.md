<p><p>The commercial water filters market encompasses various types, including Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems, Ultrafiltration (UF) membrane filters, and others. RO filters use a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved solids and contaminants, ensuring high purity water. UF filters operate with larger pore sizes, effectively eliminating bacteria and larger particles while retaining essential minerals. Other market types may include activated carbon filters, sediment filters, and ion exchange systems, each designed to address specific water quality issues in commercial settings, enhancing safety and taste.</p></p>
