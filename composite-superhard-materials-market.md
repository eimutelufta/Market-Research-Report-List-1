<p><p>The composite superhard materials market primarily includes two key types: polycrystalline diamond (PCD) and polycrystalline cubic boron nitride (PCBN). PCD is known for its exceptional hardness and thermal conductivity, making it ideal for cutting tools and drill bits in various industries. PCBN, while slightly less hard, offers superior wear resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for machining hardened steels and difficult-to-cut materials. Both types are essential in enhancing productivity and efficiency across manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
