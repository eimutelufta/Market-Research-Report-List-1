<p><p>The multifunctional vibration conveyor market comprises two main types: natural frequency and forced vibration conveyors. Natural frequency conveyors rely on the system's inherent resonance to move materials, resulting in energy efficiency and reduced power consumption. In contrast, forced vibration conveyors utilize an external mechanical force to create consistent vibrations, enabling controlled material flow regardless of mass. Both types are versatile, serving various industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and mining, offering solutions for efficient material handling and processing.</p></p>
