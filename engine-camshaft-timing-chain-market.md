<p><p>The engine camshaft timing chain market is primarily categorized into two types: rubber and metal chains. Rubber timing chains are lightweight, flexible, and typically quieter during operation, making them suitable for specific engine designs. Conversely, metal chains are more robust and durable, providing enhanced longevity and reliability under demanding conditions.
