<p><p>Urostomy pouches are essential products for individuals with urinary diversions, offering options like one-piece and two-piece systems. In contrast, two-piece pouches consist of a separate adhesive barrier and pouch, allowing for flexible pouch replacement while maintaining the skin barrier. This modular design facilitates comfort and convenience, catering to individual preferences and needs in the urostomy market.</p></p>
