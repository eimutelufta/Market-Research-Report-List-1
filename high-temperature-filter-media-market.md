<p><p>The high-temperature filter media market includes several types, each designed for specific applications. PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) offers excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability. P84, a polyimide, is known for its high thermal resistance and filtration efficiency. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is non-stick and durable, ideal for aggressive environments. Nomex, a type of aramid fiber, excels in flame resistance. PSA (Pressure-Sensitive Adhesive) media provide effective filtration with adhesion properties. Fiber glass is used for its strength and thermal resilience, making it suitable for high-temperature applications.</p></p>
