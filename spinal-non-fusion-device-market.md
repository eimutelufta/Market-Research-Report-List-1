<p><p>The spinal non-fusion device market encompasses two primary types: metal non-fusion devices and polymer non-fusion devices. Metal non-fusion devices utilize materials like titanium or stainless steel, providing strength and durability for spinal stabilization without fusion. In contrast, polymer non-fusion devices, made from biocompatible polymers, offer flexibility and lightweight options that can improve patient mobility and reduce stress on adjacent structures. Both types aim to alleviate pain and maintain spinal functionality while promoting healing and motion.</p></p>
