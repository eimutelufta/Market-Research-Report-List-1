<p><strong>The Thermal Expansion Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internally Equalized</li><li>Externally Equalized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Expansion Valves (TXVs) regulate refrigerant flow based on temperature changes in HVAC systems. Internally Equalized TXVs have internal mechanisms that balance pressure within the valve, making them suitable for smaller applications. Externally Equalized TXVs, however, feature an external sensing bulb that monitors the evaporator pressure, allowing for more accurate control in larger or more complex systems. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the refrigeration or air conditioning system and its operational demands.</p></p>
